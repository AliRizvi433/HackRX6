Compliance Terms & Conditions - Rev. 3.2  
Applicable to: All internal departments and external vendors  

1. Data Security:  
- All systems must adhere to ISO 27001 standards.  
- Data encryption at rest and in transit is mandatory.  

2. Access Control:  
- User roles must be clearly defined. Admin privileges must be audited quarterly.  

3. Policy Violation:  
- Non-compliance may result in termination of access and disciplinary actions.  
- Incidents must be reported within 24 hours.  

4. Vendor Contracts:  
- All vendor agreements must include SLA clauses and data protection addenda.
